The Challenge

Why Supply Chains Break

Modern supply chains are reactive, fragmented, and unable to act autonomously — causing billions in losses annually.

📉
Demand Volatility
Manual forecasting fails to capture real-time shifts, leading to stockouts and overstock scenarios.
⚠️
Supplier Risk Blindspot
No proactive risk scoring — financial, quality, and geopolitical threats go undetected until it's too late.
🏭
Inventory Imbalance
Warehouses operate in silos, resulting in misallocated stock, high carrying costs, and fulfillment gaps.
🚚
Logistics Inefficiency
Route decisions made manually, missing dynamic cost vs speed optimisation and shipment delay prediction.
💸
Rising Operational Costs
Procurement bottlenecks and maverick spending waste 12–20% of supply chain budgets annually.
🌍
Sustainability Gaps
No framework to track environmental impact across suppliers, logistics, and warehouse operations.

📦DatasetsMock Data Engine
GET REQUESTS → DATASETS.PY

Dashboard KPIs, warehouse data, product lists bypass AI entirely — served directly from Pandas DataFrames for high performance.

POST REQUESTS → LANGCHAIN

Supplier risk analysis and orchestrator tasks trigger the full LangChain agent graph with Ollama LLM reasoning.

AGENT STATES → POLLING (5s)

Frontend polls /api/agents/states every 5 sec. Agents cycle through IDLE → THINKING → ACTING → WAITING.

FALLBACK MODE

If Ollama is offline, system degrades gracefully to intelligent mock responses — zero downtime for UI flows.
